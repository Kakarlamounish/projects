Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science focused on creating smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding. Key components of AI include machine learning, which trains algorithms to make predictions without explicit programming; deep learning, which uses neural networks to model complex patterns; natural language processing, which enables computers to understand and generate human language; computer vision, which allows machines to interpret visual data; and robotics, which designs and operates autonomous machines. AI has wide-ranging applications in healthcare, finance, transportation, entertainment, and customer service, but it also raises ethical considerations such as bias, privacy, transparency, and job displacement.
